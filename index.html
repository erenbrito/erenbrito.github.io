Eren Brito

Web Developer

As a freelance front-end developer, I bring a strong foundation in web development, project management, and team leadership. In my previous role as Technology Coordinator, I managed a diverse portfolio of projects, overseeing task distribution, prioritization, and effort estimation using Jira and Microsoft Teams to streamline workflows. I actively participated in recruitment, conducting interviews for roles across Frontend, Backend, and Intern positions, and acted as a liaison between the Customer Service and Development teams to facilitate thorough documentation and alignment on customer requirements.

Technically, I have developed and maintained responsive, scalable pages using TypeScript, HTML Handlebars, Sass, and Bootstrap. My experience spans across multiple CMS platforms, notably WordPress, Vignette, and Acquia, with a particular focus on Shopify, where I have worked on customizing themes, integrating plugins, and creating e-commerce solutions tailored to business needs. I am skilled in using Swiper for interactive content displays and employ componentization practices to enhance code reusability and reduce redundancy. Additionally, I have built responsive websites, landing pages, and e-commerce sites using PHP, HTML, CSS (Sass), and JavaScript (jQuery), leveraging plugins like WooCommerce, ACF, and LearnPress to elevate functionality.

In my project management toolkit, I regularly utilize Trello, ClickUp, and Asana for self-organization and team collaboration, with Git version control managed through Bitbucket. I have experience implementing complex functionalities, such as custom filtering logic, managing cookies, dynamic content with jQuery, and adapting plugins like Flatpickr and Swiper. My technical acumen extends to optimizing for responsive design, cross-browser compatibility, and performance in content-heavy or e-commerce environments.
